Music

At the start of High School, my musical spirit blossomed, and I began to teach myself instruments and music production. I found people with similiar interests and music talent, and began making music. I also found I enjoyed the performance and production of live music and occasionally hold some concerts and jams.









At the end of High School, I considered a career in music production and interned at Coupe Studios in Boulder, Colorado. It was a great experience, and I learned a lot. One of the takeaways was to not pursue formal education in music rather to work on it on the side.
