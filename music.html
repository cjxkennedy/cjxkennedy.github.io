Music

Music Career

At the start of High School, my musical spirit blossomed, and I began to teach myself instruments and music production. I found people with similiar interests and music talent, and began making music. I also found I enjoyed the performance and production of live music and occasionally hold some concerts and jams.









At the end of High School, I considered a career in music production and interned at Coupe Studios in Boulder, Colorado. I was fortunate to work along with engineer/producer Taylor Marvin and get a hands on feel. It was a great experience, and I learned a lot. One of the takeaways was to not pursue formal education in music rather to work on it on the side.

Music Technology

I have strong interest in the overlap between technology and music creation and performance. I have a solid understanding of synthesis and audio processing. However, I have some of the greatest interest in live electronic performances. Specifically, using DMX lighting software with an integrated system to sequence light patterns over USB!
