Matlab Projects

Orbital Mechanics
Constellation Design

After study of Orbital Mechanics, teams aimed to design and present a constellation of satelites that would satsify as many "customers" as possible. Prior to this, I created a Matlab script that propagates a spacecraft constellation for a full mean solar day while testing line-of-sight of each spacecraft. The script also created a 3D plot of the fully propagated constellation orbit with major Earth's cities and coastlines.

My script was chosen for the rendering and the design parameters were assessed.

We performed optimization and designed the constellation.

Aerodynamics
Computational Assignments

During my Aerodynamics class, I created a variety of Matlab scripts calculating and visualizing simplified aerodynamics. There are three main assignments with a variety of topics such as analysis of flow around various airfoils, simulated performance, and various definite integral approximations.

My favorite was visualizing flow thick airfoils. Specifically, presenting the thick airfoil pressure contours.

We can also look at thin airfoils.
