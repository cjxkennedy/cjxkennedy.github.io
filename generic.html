Orbital Mechanics
Constellation Design

After study of Orbital Mechanics, teams aimed to design and present a constellation of satelites that would satsify as many "customers" as possible. Prior to this, I created a Matlab script that propagates a spacecraft constellation for a full mean solar day while testing line-of-sight of each spacecraft. The script also created a 3D plot of the fully propagated constellation orbit with major Earth's cities and coastlines. We ended up using my MATLAB code for the rendering.

Aerodynamics
Computational Assignments

During my Aerodynamics class, I created a variety of Matlab scripts calculating and visualizing simplified aerodynamics. There are three main assignments with a variety of topics such as analysis of flow around various airfoils, simulated performance, and various definite integral approximations.

My favorite was visualizing flow thick airfoils. Specifically, presenting the thick airfoil pressure contours.

We can also look at thin airfoils.

Matlab Apps
NSIV - GUIS

Matlab Apps allows for the simple design and devlepment of fully functional applications. In my NANOSAMIV project, I used Matlab to create Apps that can communicate with a microcontroller over USB. Furthermore, this command handling is controlled a variety of different ways but simplifies to sending a start and stop command. The app is able to stay in a data collection loop while able to be interrupted to stop collection.

The DataFunction GUI has processing functions to clean up, parse, and convert the data to a more usable form. After that, the data can be viewed by selecting one of many graphs for quick verification.
