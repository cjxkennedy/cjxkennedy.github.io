Hi, I'm CJ Kennedy!

I am a passionate engineer, computer scientist, and music producer.

Welcome to my page! My name is CJ Kennedy, and I am graduating Senior with a Bachelor's in Aerospace Engineering Sciences and a minor in Computer Science. I have always been drawn to the intersection of hardware and software, and I find that this combination allows for the most innovative and exciting developments and projects. My educational experience has inspired me to pursue the Aerospace and Computer Science industries.

In my academic pursuits, I have found that I am particularly passionate about hands-on engineering, embedded systems, and software development. These fields have allowed me to work on projects that range from constructing electronic boards* to developing embedded software on microcontrollers. I thrive on challenging projects that require creativity, critical thinking, and technical skills. While I enjoy working in traditional team roles, I am always willing to step up and take the lead when the need arises. I am hardworking, dependable, and have a natural ability to motivate and uplift those around me. My passion for my work extends beyond the classroom, as I am also an avid music producer. I find that music allows me to express my creativity in a different way, and it provides a balance to my technical pursuits.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit more about me. I look forward to connecting with you and exploring opportunities to collaborate in the future!
